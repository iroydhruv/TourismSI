Welcome to Lakshadweep & Puducherry

Lakshadweep, a tropical paradise, is known for its crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and untouched islands. Puducherry, with its French colonial charm, serene beaches, and spiritual centers, offers a unique cultural blend. Together, they promise a journey of tranquility and adventure.

Top Places to Visit

Agatti Island

Known for its coral reefs, lagoons, and clear waters, Agatti is perfect for snorkeling, scuba diving, and kayaking.

Bangaram Island

A secluded paradise with pristine beaches, perfect for honeymooners and adventure seekers.

Kavaratti Island

The capital of Lakshadweep, Kavaratti is known for its beautiful mosques, marine life, and lagoons.

French Quarter

Charming colonial architecture, vibrant cafes, and tree-lined streets make the French Quarter a must-visit.

Auroville

A unique township dedicated to peace and unity, home to the iconic golden Matrimandir.

Promenade Beach

A popular seaside stretch, perfect for evening walks, sunrise views, and relaxation by the sea.

Best Time to Visit

-> October to March is the ideal time to visit both Lakshadweep and Puducherry, with pleasant weather. April–June is warm, but beach lovers can still enjoy water sports and sea breezes. July–September sees monsoon rains, best for travelers seeking a calm, less-crowded experience.
